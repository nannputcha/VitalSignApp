A System for Vital Signs Self-Measurement

The patients can take thier daily vital signs measurement and send it to medical assistants to keep track of patients’ conditions.

How It Works?

 The hospital will asks for the cooperation from the patients who have been evaluated that they are still be able to take thier own vital signs to perform the task under supervision.

1
It is time for vital signs check. The patients can scan their own barcode to log into the system.
2
The patients measure their blood pressure, pulse rate, etc., and can easily enter the data in VitalSign App.
3
Medical assistants will receive the patients’ data and can monitor in a faster and more conveninent way.
4
With the patients help, doctors and assistants will have more time to take care emergency cases.
